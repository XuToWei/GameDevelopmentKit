------------------------------------------
QUICK START
------------------------------------------
To get started creating a cutscene:
1. Navigate to "Tools/SLATE/Open SLATE".
2. Click "New Cutscene".
3. Press the "Play" button in the SLATE editor window or scrub by dragging within the timebar at the top.
4. Drag and Drop GameObjects or Prefabs you wish to animate in the editor window.
5. Right Click or press the "+" button on the groups on the left to add new tracks.
6. Right Click in the track timelines to add new clips.
7. Remember to add the preafab named '@DirectorGUI' in the scene (found in the root Slate folder).
8. Please visit the website for full documentation and downloads at: https://slate.paradoxnotion.com to get the most out of using SLATE!
